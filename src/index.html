PROBLEM

Inaccessibility of art market

  • Most artworks are not for sale

  • High investment ($250k+) and low liquidity (5+ year lockups)

Lack of transparency

  • Difficult to track ownership provenance fraud 15% - 40% of art market

  • High fees reduce profits for investors by 40 - 50%

MARKET & BUSINESS

Art market offers a sizeable market opportunity. The current art market is $50bn, but we focus initially on capturing the Art Funds market ($1.2bn) and then scaling to the new wealth/funds (estimated $100bn) that will come into art markets in the next 5 years due to the biggest ever growth in the number of High Net Worth Individuals.

Orion Vault offers unique opportunity to museums to put to work their vast collections that are mostly in storage. The physical art in museums collections is not for sale, however museums are able to create with OV the Digital Masterpieces, a separate from physical art, digital entity and offer it to donors and investors. This has potential of unlocking the $1tn of stored art.
